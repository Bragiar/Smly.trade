Smileycoin is a open source peer-to-peer digital currency used for rewarding students for their performance in the tutor-web learning environment

What is this digital currency thing?

The Smileycoin works just like other electronic currencies such as Bitcoin, Litecoin or Dogecoin. It was introduced as a reward to be used in an on-line educational system.

What can you do with Smileycoin?

Buy Airline coupons.
Buy coupons for the icelandic cinema.
Pay tutors within the tutor-web system
Buy coffee on the University of Iceland campus
Play simple games, like double or nothing game
Sell SMLY at a currency exchange.

The simplest game of all is the SMLY double-or-nothing game:


Deposit any (small) amount of SMLY to the BEtZyyY address BEtZyyYqDXqmRJJ45nnL15cuASfiXg9Yik and you will automatically participate in a double-or-nothing game.

If you are on a Windows, Linux or Mac computer then the easiest way to do this is in the Console window, where you can give commands of the form:

sendtoaddress BEtZyyYqDXqmRJJ45nnL15cuASfiXg9Yik 20

repeatedly and see how you sometimes get the double back and sometimes nothing.
